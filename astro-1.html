The Emperor and Empress. Apollo and Dionysus

From Robert Wang "When we close our eyes and allow free-form images to float by us. Taking whatever direction they will, we are tapping into the life energy "ruled" by THE EMPRESS. When we begin to think about what is happening before our eyes, classifying the images according to color, subject, or any other criterion, we are calling THE EMPEROR into play. We are acting upon form.
